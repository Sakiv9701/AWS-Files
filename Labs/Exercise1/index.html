About Us

Our DevOps methodology refers to anything that Influences the interaction between development and operations. Development needs as many changes as possible to grow and to meet the needs of the changing time while for Operations change is the enemy. Operations require stability and thus strongly resist change, this is a requirement for Development.

Cloud

Cloud-based DevOps lessens the need to account for resources leveraged. Clouds leverage usage-based accounting, which tracks the use of resources by application, developer, user, data, etc. Traditional systems typically don't provide this service.

Containerization



Containers are abstractions of software like virtual machines are of hardware. Containerized environments are lightweight, with each container including all the dependencies required for the software to operate.
